<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="🦅 CodeHawks - Competitive Audits" icon="shield-halved">

    **Features:**
    - Competitive audit contests with prize pools
    - First Flights for beginners
    - Cyfrin Eagles elite program
    - Private audits for protocols
    - Transparent judging and appeals process

  <Accordion title="🎓 Updraft - Web3 Education" icon="graduation-cap">

    - Hands-on coding challenges
    - Professional certifications
    - Study guides and exam prep
    - Industry-recognized credentials

    - Personal note-taking
    - Community-driven ratings

    - Wallet integration
    - Two-factor authentication
    - Third-party platform connections
    - Customizable settings

    [Manage Profile →](/profiles/overview)
